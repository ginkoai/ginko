ginko charter

Your Blueprint For Success

ginko charter gives your AI partner the big picture: project goals, audiences, pain-points, architecture, scope, acceptance criteria and more, all using natural conversation.

WHY-WHAT-HOW

ginko epic creates your work breakdown structure from your charter, knowledge docs, and conversation with you. Epics are broken down into sprints and tasks to keep your work on track.
