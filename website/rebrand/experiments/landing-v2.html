ginko start

Ready Player YOU

ginko start loads your session context with tasks, goals, knowledge, and everything important you did in your last session. No more re-explaining the basics to your AI partner, just straight back to flow in 30 seconds or less.

ginko charter

Your Blueprint For Success

ginko charter gives your AI partner the big picture: project goals, audiences, pain-points, architecture, scope, acceptance criteria and more, all using natural conversation.

ginko epic

WHY-WHAT-HOW

ginko epic creates your work breakdown structure from your charter, knowledge docs, and conversation with you. Epics are broken down into sprints and tasks to keep your work on track.

ginko vibecheck

Take A Beat

ginko vibecheck is a built-in behavior that gives you and your AI partner a blame-free moment to challenge approach and pivot back to flow. Simply call "vibecheck" whenever things seem off, then have an open conversation.

ginko insights

Level Up

ginko insights analyzes your session logs, commits, and other data to pinpoint exactly where you are effective and where you have opportunities to improve. Team leaders can use ginko insights to level-up their whole team with measurable results.

ginko handoff

We'll Take It From Here

ginko handoff handles all the end-of-session housekeeping in a single command. Git commits, temp-file cleanup, doc updates and more are all taken care of so you can start your next session right where you left off.

ginko graph

AI-driven development takes days, not weeks. Fast-moving teams need real-time updates on task completion, issues, and insights. Ginko collaboration graph keeps all your project status updated automatically so human and AI partners all stay in sync.

FAQ

Questions?

Is my code safe? +
Yes. All context is stored locally in your .ginko folder within your project. Nothing leaves your machine unless you explicitly push to a remote repository. You own your data.
Does it work with Cursor, Copilot, or Claude? +
ginko CLI works with any AI model or IDE. It's model-agnostic and integrates seamlessly with Cursor, VS Code, JetBrains, and any tool that can read your project files.
How is this different from just using CLAUDE.md? +
CLAUDE.md is static. ginko captures dynamic session context: what you worked on, decisions made, patterns discovered, and handoff notes. It's the difference between a resume and a living memory.
What happens when I switch branches? +
ginko context travels with your branch. Each branch can have its own context state, so switching branches loads the appropriate context automatically.
Can I use ginko on multiple machines? +
Yes. Since context is stored in git, it syncs across all your machines automatically when you pull your repo. Your AI partner picks up right where you left off.
How do team permissions work? +
Team features use our collaboration graph. Admins can control who sees what insights and manage team-wide patterns. Enterprise plans include SSO and advanced access controls.
