Our Heritage

Three Generations of Automotive Excellence

Founded by master mechanic Robert Mitchell in 1978, Heritage Motors Restoration began in a two-bay garage with a simple mission: restore classic American automobiles to their former glory using time-honored techniques and uncompromising standards.

Today, our 15,000 sq. ft. facility houses a team of certified master technicians, state-of-the-art equipment, and the same dedication to excellence that built our reputation. We've restored over 500 classic vehicles, from Corvettes to Cadillacs, earning recognition at Pebble Beach, SEMA, and countless local shows.
