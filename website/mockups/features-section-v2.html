Option A: Horizontal Scroll + Rainbow Connector

Cards scroll horizontally, rainbow line flows behind connecting all features. Diagonal stripe background.

HOW GINKO WORKS

01
01

ginko start

Ready Player YOU

Loads your session context with tasks, goals, knowledge, and everything important from your last session. Back to flow in 30 seconds.

02
02

ginko charter

Your Blueprint For Success

Give your AI the big picture: project goals, audiences, pain-points, architecture, scope, and acceptance criteria through natural conversation.

03
03

ginko epic

WHY-WHAT-HOW

Creates your work breakdown structure from your charter and conversation. Epics become sprints and tasks to keep work on track.

04
04

ginko vibecheck

Take A Beat

A blame-free moment to challenge approach and pivot back to flow. Just call "vibecheck" whenever things feel off.

05
05

ginko insights

Level Up

Analyzes your session logs, commits, and data to pinpoint where you're effective and where you can improve.

06
06

ginko handoff

We'll Take It From Here

Handles all end-of-session housekeeping in one command. Git commits, temp-file cleanup, doc updates—all done.

07
07

ginko collab graph

Get Real, In Real Time

AI development moves fast. The collaboration graph keeps your whole team in sync with real-time task updates and insights.

Design Elements Used

Background patterns: Diagonal stripes (Option A), Dot grid (Option B)
Oversized numbers: Ghosted 01-07 behind each card
Rainbow connector: Vertical line (B), Horizontal bar (C)
Custom icons: Geometric vintage style with offset shadows
Colors: Sunset gradient dots + number colors matching position
